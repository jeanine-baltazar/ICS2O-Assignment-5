Description

In general, engineers use scientific discoveries in order to create useful materials and technology for people to use, and benefit from. They link scientific and mathematical principles to create these materials which can also solve technical problems and make actions more efficient.

Required Education

In order to become an engineer, you must earn at least a bachelor's degree in engineering which majors in your branch of interest. For certain fields, a masters may be required to advance.

Future Possibilities

Engineering branches off into six main categories; chemical, civil, mechanical, electrical engineering, management, and geotechnical. These six categories also have many sub categories. Therefore, there are many different careers and future possibilities for a job in engineering. For example, some of the highest paying engineering jobs include petroleum engineering, aerospace engineering, materials engineering, electrical engineering and chemical engineering.
